About Me

Hello! I'm Richard Mugisha, a passionate full-stack developer and mobile application engineer specializing in building scalable digital solutions for businesses and organizations.

I have extensive experience developing cross-platform mobile applications using Flutter and Dart for both Android and iOS, as well as building powerful backend systems using Laravel (PHP) and cloud services like Firebase.

My work focuses on creating reliable, high-performance, and user-friendly applications that solve real business challenges. I have successfully developed and launched several production applications currently used by companies and users across Uganda, Kenya, Ethiopia, South Africa, and the United Kingdom.

In addition to mobile development, I design and deploy modern websites and backend systems, ensuring seamless integration between mobile apps, servers, and cloud infrastructure.
